Welcome to Authorize

Authorize aims to keep authors and journalists honest. We value transparency and want to mitigate the amount of fake news that plagues the internet.

Our browswer plugin scans every webpage you read for information about the author, if there is one listed. We offer two versions of our plugin that provide different levels of features

Free Version

  • Author credibility score (out of 10)
  • News source credibility rating

Paid Version

  • All free version features
  • Insights into authors historical political stance based on previous work
  • MLA and ALA page citations for using the source
